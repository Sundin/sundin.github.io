The Ethics of Software Engineering

Gustav Sundin
2019-08-20
Mindset
I believe software developers have just as high ethical resposibilities as engineers within other disciplines. Many engineers (and other professionals) do for example not want to work with weapons, animal experiments and so on. My opinion is that you should consider the ethical implications of your work even if you are within a more “mundane” field such as e-commerce or game development. This is especially important considering the fact that we are just a limited number of people with the knowledge how to develop the applications that are used by the majority of the world’s population.
